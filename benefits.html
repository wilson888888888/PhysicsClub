Benefits

While we quench the thrist for knowledge and have fun in the process, here are some material benefits of being part of VC Physics Club.

Participate in Exciting Experiments

Each week physics club brings a new and exciting experiment to the members. All experiments study physics phenomenon not covered in the conventional physics classroom.

Participate in AAPT Physics Olympiad

Each year, AAPT and the American Institute of Physics (AIP) sponsor a competition for high school students. Top scoring students will represent the United States at the International Physics Olympiad.

Get AMSE Credit

Physics Club a part of AMSE program. Club members can earn AMSE credit by attending meetings and participating in activities.
